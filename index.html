I Don’t Use Social-Media, But I’m Not Anti-Social

I don’t go to social-media,

I write a story about Jeatah.

I’m a girl who likes her privacy,

Yet I love to be with my family.

I communicate through phone-call,

But with classmates and friends, not at all.

I send lists, photos, and messages through text,

If I can’t call, I do that next.

There are times I do things off-screen,

To be with my family and learn games we’ve never seen.
